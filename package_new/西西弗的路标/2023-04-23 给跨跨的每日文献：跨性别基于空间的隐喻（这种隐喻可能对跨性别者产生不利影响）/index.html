给跨跨的每日文献：跨性别基于空间的隐喻（这种隐喻可能对跨性别者产生不利影响）

原创 猫黑先生 西西弗的路标
2023年04月23日 11:56 北京

［给跨跨的每日文献］
“In English-speaking societies, the assignment of
gender is discussed as if located in a bounded region; English speakers use
terms like cross-dressing, transitioning, changing, male-to-female, coming out,
intersex. This language is indicative of a dual or binary category model of
gender assignment, in which each category is understood as a bounded region in
space. Although much of English discourse concerning gender centers on a
spatially based understanding of bounded regions and movement between, deeper probing
reveals that not all conceptions of journey through space involve the same
trajectory, direction, speed, and continuity.”
“在讲英语的社会中，性别分配的讨论就好像位于一个有界限的区域;说英语的人会使用诸如变装、过渡、改变、男变女、出柜、双性人等术语。这种语言表明了性别分配的双重或二元类别模型，其中每个类别都被理解为空间中的有界区域。尽管英语中许多关于性别的话语都是以空间为基础来理解有界限的区域和区域之间的运动，但更深入的探索表明，并非所有关于空间旅行的概念都包含相同的轨迹、方向、速度和连续性。”
——《Exploring the Metaphorical Models of Transgenderism》
文献来源：Lederer, J. (2015). Exploring the
Metaphorical Models of Transgenderism. Metaphor and Symbol, 30(2),
95–117.  https://doi.org/10.1080/10926488.2015.1016809
