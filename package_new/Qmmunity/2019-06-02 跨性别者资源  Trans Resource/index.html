荷尔蒙替代疗法

Hormone Replacement Therapy

黑市上有很多销售HRT（荷尔蒙替代疗法）药物的渠道，但大部分制造过程极不规范，会对人体造成极大的伤害，危险性极高。有些药物会谎称能“改善皮肤状态，但不会导致乳房变大的副作用”。这是骗人的的！如果您遇到任何像这样的问题，请给我们发邮件，我们会帮你咨询美国医生，给您回复。另外，千万不要通过注射硅胶改变身形，此做法已导致多人丧命。如果您想要改变您的身形，请参照本文内的变性手术板块，里面包含正规医院的信息。

There are many ways to get HRT off the black market. This is extremely dangerous as many of these pills are not scientifically vetted and can cause great harm. There are many myths about HRT such as "you can take them to improve your skin but not grow breasts." This is not true! If you have any questions about such claims, please email us, trans@theqmmunity.com, and we can get you an answer from a US doctor. In addition, you should never take silicone injections to get curves. People have died because of these injections. If you want to change the shape of your body, please refer to the Gender Reassignment Surgery section of this resource, where the hospitals we recommend offer safe ways to modify the shape of your body.

If you are looking for legal, affordable, and safe estrogen or spironolactone in Shanghai or Jiangsu Province please email this address: trans@theqmmunity.com


收到邮件后我会向您发送以下问卷：

After receiving the email I will reply:

为了进一步了解您的情况，请您回答以下问题：

To get more info about your circumstance, could you please answer these questions:

1

如果您已经开始了HRT（荷尔蒙替代疗法），是什么时候开始的呢？

If you have already started HRT, when did you start?

2

如果您已经开始了HRT（荷尔蒙替代疗法），是从医生那里得到这些激素/药物的吗？

If you have already started HRT, did you get them from a doctor?

3

如果您已经开始了HRT（荷尔蒙替代疗法），您每日的雌二醇和螺内酯剂量是多少呢？

If you have already started HRT, what is your daily dose of estradiol and spironolactone?

4

您是否有血栓或者钾含量异常的家族疾病史？

Do you have a family history of blood clots or unusual potassium levels?

5

您抽烟吗？如果抽，抽多少？

Do you smoke? If so, how much?

6

您知道如果进行HRT（荷尔蒙替代疗法），血栓风险会上升吗？如果您吸烟，则这个风险还会更高，您知道吗？

Are you aware that you will be at a much higher risk for blood clots if you take HRT, and even higher risk if you smoke?

7

您知道血栓会致死、导致变性失败吗？

Are you aware that a blood clot could kill you or prevent you from transitioning?

8

您知道血栓的前兆是什么吗？有哪些？

Do you know the warning signs of a blood clot? If so, what are they?

9

您知道您将来会失去生育能力吗？

Are you aware that you will become infertile?

10

您多大了？

How old are you?

11

您知道每个人接受HRT后的效果都因人而异，且您需要从低剂量开始注射然后慢慢加大剂量吗？加大的幅度也因人而异，您应该规律地记录您血液里的雌激素和钾含量以确保您的身体能稳定承受HRT。

Are you aware that HRT works differently for everyone, and that you need to start at a low dose, and increase your dosage slowly. This rate is different for everyone and you should regularly check your estrogen and potassium levels in your blood to ensure that your body is adapting to the HRT in a stable manner.

12

您是否愿意自行承担此过程中所有风险？您是否做过大量功课，阅读过各种来源的信息和文章？

Do you agree that the ultimate decision on this risk rests on you and that you have heavily researched this topic from multiple sources?

来询者回复上述问题后，我会告诉他们怎么合法（其实是利用法律漏洞）安全地获得HRT疗法（安全性可由美国医生认可）。

Once the reply is set, I will tell them how to obtain HRT legally (it is more of a loophole) and safely (confirmed by my US doctor).

变性手术中介

Gender Reassignment Surgery: Agents

因为在中国拿到GRS超乎想象的困难，我们推荐去泰国，这样更安全也更便宜。我们的一位志愿跟这家医院有联系，而且亲测过该医院的服务：http://pai.co.th/

Due to the fact that obtaining GRS in China is incredibly difficult, we recommend that travelling to Thailand is safer and more affordable. One of our volunteers has connections and personal experience with this hospital: http://pai.co.th/

如果您给我们发邮件，trans@theqmmunity.com,我们会为您联络PAI医院的泰国代理人并用英文或中文联系您。

If you email our address,trans@theqmmunity.com we will connect with you either an English or Mandarin speaking Thai agent of the PAI hospital. 

变性手术：激光除毛

Gender Reassignment Surgery: Laser Hair Removal

PAI医院要求手术前提前进行电解或激光脱毛。您可以用英语或中文联系百汇医疗的周珺医生。

One requirement of the PAI hospital is to undergo electrolysis or laser hair removal. One option that is available in English and Mandarin is Dr. Alva Zhou at Parkway Health Gleneagles Medical and Surgical Centre.:

南京西路389号,(近黄陂北路),明天广场裙房4楼,上海 Telephone: 400 819 6622 

389 W.Nanjing Rd (near North HuangPi Lu,People Square), Tomorrow Square, Lvl 4, Shanghai. 

我们的一位志愿者接触过周医生，没有在周珺医生和她的护士那里受到歧视或骚扰。激光除毛的第一疗程花费2000人民币（若无医保）。每个疗程开销比上一疗程减少20%。

One of our volunteers experienced no discrimination or harassment from Dr. Alva Zhou or her nurse. The first session of laser hair removal costs (without insurance) RMB 2,000. Each session after that is offered at a 20% reduced rate.

另外还可以去上海第九医院进行激光除毛（该医院医生只会说中文，不会英文）。我们不知道他们是否友跨：
