A Message to the Community: 

春节一天天临近，社群内的朋友们纷纷踏上返乡路。但回家后，难免要面临家人催婚的苦恼。

Chinese New Year is upon us, and each year many from our community travel back to their hometowns, afraid to deal with the questions that their families will ask of them. 


但正是在这个时候，我们最须要认同自己的身份。

Embrace your identity. 


从小到大，社会教育我们遵循性别二元论，家人教导我们以主流观念为行为准绳，不得越界。

We were raised in a binary society: many of our families believe we must abide by the boundaries of public opinion. 


任何少数群体都有重重障碍要克服，我们也不例外。当家人的意愿和我们的身份发生冲突，必然导致痛苦。但不能让痛苦占据我们的视野。

Just as with any diverse group of people, there are challenges we must overcome. There are misunderstandings between what our families want, and who we are. Those misunderstandings are often the source of a lot of pain, but let us not be defined by that pain. 


在异性恋霸权的社会，我们时刻感到无力又孤独，却必须选择反抗、选择寂寞。然而，也许无力仅仅是一种感觉，我们的力量比想象中的要强大不少。

A heteronormative society guides us down a path of resistance and solitude: we feel powerless, we feel ignored… but actually, we are not as powerless as it at times can feel. 


星巴克近日推出彩虹杯，正是印证了性少数群体的巨大潜力。中国性少数群体代表着高达9380亿美元的消费市场，经济实力不可小觑。但更重要的是，我们还能给当下非黑即白的社会带来一抹色彩，让中国不负世界期待，终于走向开放、走向包容。

The recently released Starbucks rainbow cups are a testimony to our strength as a community. We are worth a lot… monetarily: the value of our community in China is US 938 Billion Dollars per year. But besides our monetary power, we are also worth a lot, as bringing color to this black and white world, as representing the direction of a new, more open, and welcoming China that the world has been waiting for. 


中国是一个沉睡的巨人。让她睡着吧！因为当她醒来时, 她将震撼世界。——拿破仑

“China is a sleeping giant. Let her sleep, for when she wakes she will move the world.” - Napolean


中国，正在走向觉醒

China is waking up…


无论春节假期发生什么，请记住：你不是孤军奋战。全国上下，还有9000万性少数人士，面临同样的处境，和你在一起。

No matter what happens during your visit home, please know, you are not alone. You are part of 90 million others all around the country.


随着新一代中国青年踏入社会，会有越来越多企业注意到我们的存在，甚至表现出所谓的“关爱”。彩虹主题广告、彩虹主题产品，的确印证了群体的力量；但我们同时有义务施展力量，引导企业为性少数群体提供实际的关怀。

As the younger generation of China slowly comes to power, we will see more and more corporations taking notice and pretending to care about us. Take each advertisement, each rainbow product, as a testimony as our strength. 


我们希望这篇文章能得到广泛分享，最终让星巴克集团听到我们的心声。在提升群体关注度、改变主流思维等方面，国内的西方企业大有可为。

It is the responsibility of our community to hold these brands responsible and to treat our demographic with care and consideration. We wrote this article to be shared with the Starbucks Corporation. These large corporations who come from the west, have a great opportunity in helping our community be seen, and change the opinions of mainstream society.


无论春节期间经历怎样的情绪波动，请记住，你不是弱者。只要勇于认同自己的身份，你的力量不可小觑。

When you are feeling alone this holiday season, know that you are not weak. Know that you are a force to be reckoned with, and all it takes is a little reckoning to release that force. Let us remind them of our power…


愿你发现自己的力量，愿你向世界勇敢地展示力量。

Let us be reminded of our power.

Intro.

彩虹旗可谓是世界上最有号召力的旗帜。社群内的人，无论国籍、无论语言，只要看到彩虹旗，就知道找到了同伴，找到了组织。

One of the world’s greatest unifying forces of the world is the rainbow. Whatever country we are in, no matter what language we speak, when our community sees the rainbow, we know: those are our people, and that we are welcome here.


然而， 彩虹旗在中国的使用并不广泛。我曾前往上海某高校举办讲座。活动前，学生们提醒由于校方命令禁止，我不能把我的标志性彩虹斗篷带进校园。于是，我只好选择了一条低调的彩虹围巾。由此可见，在中国，彩虹旗的可见度很低，有些地方甚至根本不允许展示。

The use of the rainbow, however, isn’t as common in China. At a previous lecture I gave at a university in Shanghai (purposefully not specifying where), the students told me to leave my token rainbow cape at home because rainbow flags are banned on campus. I opted for a subtle rainbow scarf instead. The rainbow visibility in China is lacking, to say the least, and in some extreme cases can be banned from being displayed at all.


虹社创始人盖比戴着低调彩虹围巾，和参加讲座的大学生合影。

Founder Gabby Gabriel posing with university students while wearing her subtle rainbow scarf.


星巴克在华推出彩虹系列产品

Starbucks Launches Rainbow Products in China


正因为此，1月17日星巴克首次推出彩虹杯，立刻吸引了LGBTQ群体的眼球。

That’s why when Starbucks launched its first ever China ‘love is love’ rainbow products on January 17, it surely captured the attention of the LGBTQ community.


我最初是通过虹社的一个微信群了解到彩虹杯的。500人的上海大群里，大家转发星巴克的宣传图片，议论纷纷：“星巴克的彩虹杯，你看到了吗？”。

I found out about the new Starbucks products in one of our Qmmunity WeChat groups. “Did you see the rainbow at Starbucks?” the day of its release a picture of the Starbucks’s Rainbow initiative was sent to one of our WeChat groups filled to the max, 500, LGBTQ people in Shanghai.


怀着好奇，我前往星巴克门店一探究竟。在店里，我看到了这样的标识。

Curious to know more about the products, I ventured to the nearest Starbucks to have a look. I found the sign and display.


“因为你看见彩虹

平淡的生活里

You came into my uneventful life


因为你的出现而色彩斑斓

and colored my heart


透过爱的光亮照射我的心

with love and light


因为有你，我看见彩虹”

and showed me the rainbow”

群成员看到图片，都激动不已，纷纷表示愿意购买。毕竟在现在的中国，愿意认同性少数群体的大企业品牌 实在是太少见了。

When the picture was sent to the group, members buzzed with excitement, ready-to-purchase, and hopeful to receive a “love is love” nod from a corporate brand, a rarity in China (for now).


虹社对中国LGBTQ群体展开问卷调查

We Surveyed the LGBTQ Community in China


鉴于此，虹社核心团队设计了一份调查问卷，以了解星巴克彩虹杯的影响，并启发进一步对话。

Our core team decided to create a survey to gauge the impact of the Starbucks initiative, and start a dialogue.


虹社创办于2014年，最初只是一个坐标上海的LGBTQ社交圈。经过几年的发展，虹社在微信群、微博上共有1.5万名成员和关注者；虹社开发的中文app试用版也积累了约4000名用户。

 虹社Qmmunity started initially as an LGBTQ social group in Shanghai back in 2014. Today, our community has 4,000 users on our Chinese app prototype , and a reach of about 15,000 LGBTQ people through our WeChat groups and Weibo Accounts all over China.



调查问卷《唇间的彩虹》共回收了约200份答卷，受访者来自8个不同的LGBTQ微信群，主要分布于上海、北京、苏州、成都、广州等城市。

Our “Taste the Rainbow” survey received 200 responses across 8 different LGBTQ WeChat groups in China residing in Shanghai, Beijing, Suzhou, Chengdu & Guangzhou.


绝大多数受访者为居住在中国境内的八零后、九零后 LGBTQ人士

The majority of the survey participants were LGBTQ millennials, all living in China. 


2019年1月18日，星巴克推出彩虹系列产品组合。截图为星巴克中国发布的官方微博：“爱的颜色不止一种”，隐晦地指向性少数群体。

Starbucks modestly released the rainbow “love is love” product set on [1] Weibo January 18, 2019, screenshot, with a caption that alluded to the LGBTQ community, “Love has more than one color.”


调查结果显示，大部分受访者支持星巴克在中国推出彩虹杯这一大胆举动

Survey results showed an overall appreciation for Starbucks’s new bold China rainbow initiative.


73%的中国受访者表示，看到彩虹产品，感到社会对自己更包容了。62%的外籍受访者表示，看到彩虹产品，感到社会对自己更包容了。

73% of Chinese LGBTQ feel more accepted by society when they see rainbow products. Meanwhile only 62% of LGBTQ expats feel more accepted when seeing rainbow products.

However, the part that struck me most was this:

99.9%来自大陆和港澳台的LGBTQ受访者表示，除了销售产品之外，希望商家能采取更多元的方式增加中国民众对性少数群体的了解

99.9% of the Chinese mainland + Hong Kong / Taiwan LGBTQ community surveyed wished that brands did more than sell rainbow products in order to raise awareness for the community.

星巴克在中国，还能做些什么？

How can Starbucks Do More in China?


LGBTQ群体迫切地希望看到切实的社会变化。推出彩虹杯以示支持，只不过是一种形式，最终的获利者只有星巴克，故也引发了不少批评的声音。正如一名受访者所说：“这是纯粹的商业推广。设计几个颜色的组合，轻而易举地就和性少数群体挂上钩了。”

The LGBTQ community is eager for real change; the Starbucks rainbow release brings critique because it seems superficial and beneficial only to Starbucks, as one commenter noted, “Pure business marketing, easy to find a link between colors and LGBT community.”


在中国，和性少数群体相关的公众活动虽然不少，但由于一些原因，组织起来较为困难，而且也不可能获得媒体关注报道，像在西方国家那样。尽管如此，企业仍然可以通过一些切实的行动改变群体的生存状况，既不须要很高调，又能带来经济利益。具体请参考我的另一篇文章《五种方式让你的企业对LGBT群体更友好》。

Publicly related LGBTQ initiatives in China can be difficult to pull off; though they do happen quite often, they just don’t receive the media attention that LGBTQ communities in the west receive. However, in my previous article, I mentioned 5 ways brands can make a real difference in China while making a profit, without making too much noise.


虹社联合虹口区心理健康相关部门，开展多元性&包容性培训。有关部门积极主办此类项目，说明中国的态度在慢慢转变。

Photo from Qmmunity LGBTQ D&I Training with Shanghai Hongkou District Mental Health Hospital, a Chinese government funded clinic. The fact that a government- funded establishment is taking these initiatives, shows a shifting-China.


企业可采取的措施包括向员工宣传包容性理念，开展以介绍性少数群体为主的多元性&包容性培训课程等。由于中国极度缺少来自正规渠道的LGBTQ信息，开展内部培训课程可以让员工正式学习相关知识，提升意识，同时又不会激起太大的公众反响。

Being vocal about inclusivity with the staff, signing up for diversity & inclusion training that focuses on the LGBTQ community. There is little to no-formal LGBTQ exposure on a formal level in China. A staff D&I training helps bring knowledge about these issues and spreads awareness, without having to make a public scene.


基于中国国情、以介绍性少数群体为主的多元性&包容性培训，是帮助大众了解群体最好的入门课。鉴于中国群体的众多特殊性及相关话题的匮乏，不能生搬硬套西方国家的培训内容——就像不能指望通过学英文，去掌握中文一样。

A great starting place for an introduction to LGBTQ in China, is an LGBTQ-focused China Diversity & Inclusion training tailored for China. Taking a Western D&I training for China, is like learning English to prepare for a Chinese language course– there are just way too many unique aspects to the Chinese LGBTQ experience that are not being talked about.


星巴克在中国的150个城市共有3600家门店，5万名员工。仅是开展内部员工教育，就必能带来不小的成效。

Starbucks is in 150 cities, 3600 locations, and has 50000 staff:  the effort of employee-education alone would be quite impactful.


另外，星巴克完全可以考虑将销售彩虹杯的部分利润捐赠给国内的LGBTQ组织。

Another method they could have considered, is having donated a % of the sales to a Chinese LGBTQ organization.


实际上，国内的每个企业都可以通过诸多方式，默默地、坚定地支持性少数群体。支持我们，并不一定要激起巨大社会反响、改变企业形象或违反法律法规。我们对企业抱着充分理解的心。看到彩虹，我们肯定会开心，但绝不会满足于只看到彩虹，看不到实际行动。

To be honest, the options for meaningful quiet action are within reach for all companies in China. There are lots of options brands can do without having to rock the boat, or worry about what legal or public relations will say. The community knows this. We are always happy to see the rainbow, but only seeing it, doesn’t quite do it for us.


结语

Conclusion


中国LGBTQ群体的购买力为9380亿美元。因此，星巴克今年1月份在中国推出彩虹系列产品，向性少数群体伸出橄榄枝，无疑是明智的举措——毕竟我们被冷落的时间实在太久了。

[3] The purchasing power of LGBTQ China is US$938 billion. Therefore a flash rainbow product in January, is a great way to attempt to romance one of the world’s greatest neglected demographics: the invisible LGBTQ community of China.


愿星巴克保持包容、多彩、进步的品牌定位，跟随中国的当代青年一起，帮助性少数群体走得更远。

We hope that as Starbucks continues to position itself as an accepting, colorful, contemporary brand, slowly pivoting with the open-minded millennial citizens of China, that it takes meaningful steps to help the community move forward.


包容性的企业文化，并不该只限于西方国家。越来越多西方企业进入中国市场，而在此同时，它们有义务将中国性少数群体迫切需要的包容性文化带到中国。

Creating an inclusive company culture isn’t just something reserved for the west. As more western companies take on the Chinese market, they should bring their inclusive company cultures with them: China’s LGBTQ Community needs it.


2018年12月，虹社为中国百威英博市场营销部门举办讲座

Image from December 2018 invitation to speak to ABInBev China's Marketing Department.


愿星巴克坚持力挺彩虹，并采取切实的行动，让每个拥有彩虹心的人都有可能做真实的自己。

We hope as Starbucks continues to position themselves as rainbow-embracing, that they take real action towards helping those rainbow hearts stay true to their colorful-selves.
