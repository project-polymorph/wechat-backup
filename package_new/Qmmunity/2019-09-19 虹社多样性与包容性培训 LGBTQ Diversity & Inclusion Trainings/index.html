Our Diversity & Inclusion 

Training Programs

      虹社致力于让更多的人理解和支持LGBTQ+社区。

      为此，我们为学校、公司和政府机构提供定制性的多元化和包容性培训服务，这些机构关注员工和学生，使其朝着包容性和多样性的方向发展，尤其是围绕关于性别或性取向的主题。


Qmmunity is committed to the advocacy and understanding of the LGBTQ+ community.


Because of this, we provide customized Diversity & Inclusion training services for schools, companies, and governmental organizations who seek to invest in their staff or students’ development towards inclusivity and diversity, particularly around topics of gender or sexual orientation.

关于虹社的多样性和包容性培训

D&I Training for Duke University Kunshan

在昆山大学与公寓管理学生会同学们分享故事

Experience sharing during the Duke University D&I Training for Residential Assistants 


     今年8月,我们在杜克大学昆山分校组织了一场关于多样性和包容性的培训,我们的培训讲师和虹社工作人员一起参与了这场令人深受启发又极具教育意义的研讨。其中包括概念介绍、情景分析、性别认同以及如何处理不同的困难情况,如性骚扰和种族歧视等。

      在我们的研讨课结束后，小组里的每一个人都受到了思维的启发和心态的开放，这次讨论也使他们更加紧密地团结在一起。


This past August, we completed D&I training session for Duke Kunshan University, where our trainers and community leaders were able to deliver a thought-provoking and eye-opening workshop that included scenario-based-training for residential assistants on the campus, an introduction of gender identity issues, and how to handle difficult  situations such as sexual harassment, and racial discrimination.


After our training session, everyone in the group felt more open minded and as if though the discussion that was had brought them all closer together.


“这是一个很棒的研讨会。今天所讨论的知识和经验是他们人生体验的开始。感谢您为我们的学生们所做的一切!! ”


“It was an amazing workshop. The knowledge, discussion and connections that RAs are able to made might even be a starting point of an life changing experience for them. Thank you for making it happen for our students!!”

陈珺JUN CHEN

大学生公寓生活协调员   

Undergraduate Residence Life Coordinator


 美国商会企业社会责任年度大会

AmCham CSR Conference 

From left to right从左至右 : Gabby Gabriel (虹社创始人 Qmmunity Founder), Ker Gibbs (美国商会主席 AmCham President), Lili Peng (虹社首席运营官 Qmmunity Operations Officer)


      9月5日，虹社应邀参加美国商会在上海举办的企业社会责任年度大会。我们有幸能够谈论我们的服务和致力于在中国发展更加包容的环境所做的努力。

      我们还有幸观看了企业社会责任(CSR)专家们的精彩演讲，他们向我们阐明了如何将企业社会责任计划应用于实际情况的方方面面。


On the 5th of September, Qmmunity was invited to the annual CSR Conference organized by the American Chamber of Commerce in Shanghai. We were able to talk about our services and dedication to developing inclusive environments amongst organizations in China.


We also had the privilege to watch amazing presentations by Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) specialists, who illuminated us on the practical aspects of applying CSR initiatives to real-life situations.


我们为什么要这么做?

Why do we do what we do?


      我们都知道，学生和员工在环境包容度上都表现得更好，尤其当他们也是LGBTQ+中的一员时。

      我们希望可以将多样性和包容性的培训以及我们讲师的专业知识带给各个机构的员工和学生，以促进更多人享受到多元化和包容性的环境中。


We all know that students and employees perform better in accepting and inclusive environments, especially if they themselves are LGBTQ+. We hope that we can bring our trainers’ and community leaders’ expertise to the people in these institutions in order to further the Diversity & Inclusion cause.
