原创 LQ Qmmunity
2018年01月16日 21:12


2018年1月12日，由LesQueers（下文简称LQ）主办，Roxie（微信公众号：roxieshanghai）和白色城堡（微信公众号：whitecastle1921）赞助的LGBTQIA+社区启动大会在WeWork成功举行。

On January 12, 2018, LesQueers (LQ) hosted a kickoff event, with Roxie and White Castle as sponsors. More than half of Shanghai’s LGBTIQ+ community organizations gathered at WeWork to announce exciting plans and innovations for the year.


图 / LGBTQIA+社区启动大会现场

LGBTQIA+ kickoff event


现场聚集了上海骄傲节、同性恋亲友会、LifeLine、OpenDoors、上海酷儿影展、CinemQ、上海女爱、我们与平权、Ladyfest、DKNSTRKT、酷儿说、不直电台等12个性少数群体公益组织，创始人们与现场观众分享了创建至今的心路历程与2018年的全新展望。

The attending organizations were Shanghai Pride, PFLAG, Lifeline, OpenDoors, SHQFF, CinemQ, Nvai, We and Equality, Ladyfest, DKNSTRKT, Queer Talks, and The Curve. All eyes were on the organizing founders as each shared insights to what 2018 has in store for the community.

LQ创始人Gabby Gabriel现场发言，讲述了LQ为推进LGBTQIA+性少数群体的可见度和包容度上所做的努力。

LQ Founder, Gabby Gabriel, spoke about how the LQ is to become a pillar of support to Shanghai’s LGBTQIA+ community organizations and will advocate on their behalf for mainstream acceptance.

LQ founder, Gabby Gabriel


在活动现场，Gabby谈到：“2012年来中国之前，她在网上搜索不到中国拉拉，也感觉不到这个东方国度的彩虹色彩，当她在上海生活2年后，2014年，她决定成立LQ，为了将容易被忽略的LGBTQ+群体聚集起来，提升群体的可见度，改变社会的包容度。”

“When I arrived in China in 2012, I couldn’t find any lesbians. After living in Shanghai for two years, I created the LQ, to gather community for regular events and to develop stronger connections within our community.”

Q Key

A Must-Have Lifestyle Guide towards Equality


迷路的时候我们查看地图，可以知道正确的方向；肚子饿的时候除了随便走进一家店碰运气，我们还可以在各种点评网站上斟酌一番再做对得起自己肚子的决定；就业时也是一样，莽莽撞撞的几轮面试无法让我们分辨这家企业的文化是否开放包容，未来的工作环境是否让人开心满意。

To better understand the businesses, venues, and organizations that are committed to a safe and welcoming environment for the LGBTIQ+ community, we have created a key that the community can use to better evaluate establishments in terms of whether to patron, to work with/at, or to support in other capacities.



在中国有2.7亿性少数群体，但只有5%的性少数人群在公司表明了自己的性取向，他们遍布各行各业，真实的存活在每个人的生活里。作为LGBTQIA+性少数文化的引领者，LQ从社群需求出发，通过为企业培训认证，帮助企业创造更包容的工作环境，增强员工的身心健康和创新精神，提升员工的工作满意度和忠诚度，为中国的商务版图贡献彩虹力量。

LesQueers is pioneering China’s LGBTIQ+ diversity and inclusion training dedicated to achieving lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender workplace equality. We partner with psychologists, companies and agencies to provide comprehensive training, consultation, and professional networking opportunities that build inclusive and welcoming work environments.


除了就业时的风向标，彩虹指南还能帮助社群在选择吃喝玩乐等营业场所时，更清晰高效的发现安全友好的目标地点。

Besides employment, Q Key can establish which businesses, venues, and organizations are committed to a safe and welcoming environment for the LGBTIQ+ community.



作为中国首家提供彩虹指南服务的社群，LQ希望彩虹指南不仅能优化中国的就业环境，提升企业的软实力，为企业打造留得住每一位优质员工的舒适环境，同时也能让每一位性少数群体用最简单的方式发现最适合自己的去处。

As China’s First benchmark guide to service the community, LQ hopes that not only the Q key can cultivate China’s existing employment environment, but also help consumers make wiser decisions.


LGBTQIA+多样性及包容度培训

提升企业软实力的最佳途径


LGBTQIA+ Diversity and Tolerance Training

The Best Way to Improve Enterprises’ Inclusiveness


针对不同的行业和工作环境，LQ为每一家参与LGBTQIA+群体多样性及包容度培训的企业提供初级、中级、高级等不同级别的高度定制化培训及咨询项目。从中国的性少数群体运动的现状起步，让每个人从了解群体开始，完成从旁观到同盟再到倡导者的角色转变。2017年12月，LQ为跨国非盈利性组织LifeLine提供了培训服务。

Aimed at different industries and work environments, LQ is providing every participant different levels of, Introductory, Intermediate, Advanced and Consulting, all tailored to industry specific needs. From China Pride, to different events in the community, we want people to understand and be aware of how the rainbow is relevant to everyone around us. November 2017, LQ provided training services to LifeLine, a multinational non-profit organization.

Lifeline workshop


定制化的标识能够将企业自动收录进彩虹指南，向员工和顾客清晰明了的传递企业的LGBTQIA+友好文化信息。

The Q Key is a symbolized guide that demonstrates five varying factors of inclusivity.


图 / 彩虹指南认证标识

The Queer Index Key


  • This organization has received Q Key LGBTIQ+ Diversity & Inclusion Training 

  • This organization is LGBTIQ+ inclusive in its hiring policy and employee benefits 

  • This organization has an LGBTIQ+ community safe marker 

  • This organization has gender expansive bathrooms 

  • This organization is queer owned, or has queer employees 


LQ也提供针对性少数群体的企业咨询服务，包括招聘和福利政策、商业咨询，性别过渡期指导，无性别洗手间建立等信息咨询。

LQ also aims to provide the community with corporate consultation services, including employment, benefit policies, sexuality transition consult, gender expansive bathroom establishment and other information services.

上海骄傲节 ShanghaiPRIDE


初创于2009年的上海骄傲节，是一年一度倡导多元化的庆典。上海骄傲节完全由志愿者运营，并获得来自媒体、企业、个人和外国领事馆的支持。我们旨在通过丰富多彩的体育、文化、教育、社交活动为LGBTQ群体提升自我认同，社会能见度与包容度。同时，上海骄傲节作为非营利节庆，集合社群与伙伴的力量，支持与LGBTQ社群相关项目的发展。

ShanghaiPRIDE, which began in 2009, is an annual festival that celebrates diversity. This festival is organized entirely by volunteers with the support of media, businesses, individuals, and foreign consulates. We aim to raise awareness and visibility, and to promote self-acceptance and acceptance for the LGBTQ community through sports, cultural, educational, and social activities. As a non-profit festival, we also encourage the community and partners to support LGBTQ-related projects and initiatives.  


2014年，上海骄傲节团队获得由上海Time Out杂志评选的“上海英雄奖”。

In 2014, the ShanghaiPRIDE team was awarded “Shanghai Hero” by Time Out Shanghai magazine.  


2018年，第十届上海骄傲节将于6月8日至18日举办。

ShanghaiPRIDE 2018 – Pride10 will be held in conjunction with the first CnPRIDE from the 8th to the 18th of June.

同性恋亲友会 PFLAG China



同性恋亲友会，英文简称PFLAG China（意为中国男女同性恋者的父母、家人和朋友），成立于2008年6月28日。目前，亲友会在国内52个城市有分会和小组，拥有同志亲友和同志志愿者3000余名，注册会员12万。通过家长之间的恳谈与分享、热线、讲座、举办各类活动等方式，鼓励同性恋者实现自我认同；促进同性恋者与其亲友间的理解沟通；向公众倡导性倾向平等，提升同性恋人群的社会可见度。

PFLAG China ( short for Parents, Families and Friends of Lesbians and Gays of China) was established on June 28, 2008. By far, it has reached over 52 cities in China, with more than 3,000 LGBT volunteers and 120,000 registered members. By means of dialogues, exchanges, helpline, lectures and other activities, PFLAG China strives to encourage LGBT people to embrace their own identity, to facilitate communication and understanding between LGBT and their families and friends, and to publicly advocate equality between sexual orientations and improve the social visibility of LGBT people.


LadyFest


Ladyfest Shanghai是一个完全由女性发起的的民间草根组织，其使命是每年举办支持女性赋权的主题庆祝活动，以促进性别平等。活动呼吁当地社区齐聚一堂，通过广泛的技能分享和交流来倡导自我表达的自由权利，通过创造一个相互启发的开放环境及促进经验交流的平台，来推动和实现性别平等社会的愿景。无论你的性别，身材，肤色，文化背景，宗教信仰，政治立场或罩杯大小是什么，Ladyfest都欢迎你加入这场女性革命，一同为提升女性社会地位而努力！

Ladyfest Shanghai is a grassroots organization formulated by a group of women with the mission to create an event that is a celebration of female empowerment and to promote a dialogue for gender equality. The annual event calls upon the local community to join in supporting freedom of self-expression through a wide diversity of skills and to create an open environment which fosters mutual inspiration, exchange of experiences and visions of femininity and equality. Regardless of gender, shape, size, color, cultural background, religious beliefs, political stance, or cup size, everyone is encouraged to be a part of the effort recognized on International Women's Day.

女爱 nvai



女爱成立于2005年，是最早成立的LGBTQ公益机构之一。关注LGBT议题，倡导性别平等的公益组织。我们希望通过更全面和公正的倡导，改变社会的歧视，积极为推进社会平等、和谐、多元而努力。

Shanghai NVAI was established in 2005, the earliest LBTGQ NGO in the community of Shanghai. It focuses not only on LBGT issues, but also plays an essential role in the feminism movements. The goal is to reduce the social discrimination and advocate social equality, diversity and harmony.


女爱在同志议题上有丰富的公益项目经验，为上海地区同志社群做过多个项目，包括 “口述历史”，“戏剧教育”，“校园倡导”，“大陆拉拉地图”及“社会行动”。

NVAI has done a great number of projects for LGBT community in Shanghai and experienced in social welfare programs, including “Oral History”,“Theatre Education”, “Chinese Lesbian Map” and “Social Action”.


女爱与及其他地区同志社群和公益组织有长久的合作，为他们提供各类项目的培训支持。 

NVAI has long term cooperations with the LGBT communities and NGOs outside of Shanghai, providing different kinds of support and training programs.


女爱正在开始拍一部上海LGBTQ十五年历程的纪录片，以影像记录梳理中国上海十五年（2003-2015）LGBTQ的发展及其参与人员对于LGBTQ和性别平等事业的推动，描绘与建构上海LGBTQ在时间与空间交织下的流变，重新回顾上海LGBTQ的历史，和集体回忆。我觉得记录历史是非常有必要的工作：纪录片透过呈现一个城市的社群记录，引发对LGBTQ运动的集体反思；另一方面纪录片的传播也将使更多人看到和认知有关性/别平等的多元议题。这个项目我们还需要很多的支持和帮助，同时我们也有做网上众筹，参与众筹还有机会成为纪录片的联合制片人，欢迎大家联系和关注我们。

We are beginning to make a journey of fifteen years in Shanghai LGBTQ documentary in Shanghai for fifteen years China combing video recording (2003-2015) to promote the development of LGBTQ and its staff for the cause of LGBTQ and gender equality, depicting the rheological and construction of Shanghai LGBTQ in time and space in the mix, review the history of Shanghai LGBTQ, and collective memory. I think recording history is a very necessary job: documentary shows a collective reflection of LGBTQ movement through presenting a city's social record. On the other hand, the spread of documentaries will also enable more people to see and cognizance of multiple issues of sex / equality. We also need a lot of support and help for this project. At the same time, we also have online public funding and participate in the crowd raising. There is still a chance to become a co producer of the documentary. 我们与平权 We and Equality



我们与平权是一个发起于2016年11月的草根项目，旨在赋权于中国女性与男性，使ta们可以自主选择自己的生活方式，婚恋方式与职业路径。通过微信公众号，我们每周推送一篇原创文章，以性别平等的视角来采访身边的人、推荐影视作品与书籍、分享关于性别议题的观点，从而引发大家对于围绕着我们的性别不平等现象的思考。我们每两周举办一次线下活动对特定性别话题进行讨论，同时也有两个微信群每天讨论不同话题，以期可以互相扶持相互帮助。我们的产品是我们传达平权信息的载体，同时也是我们目前唯一的经济来源。

"We and Equality" is a grass root project started in November 2016 dedicated on empowering Chinese women and men to choose their lifestyles regardless of gender. Through the WeChat Subscription account, we release original articles every week to raise awareness and knowledge of gender-related topics in China. The once-every-2-weeks offline activities help us create an environment for in-depth discussions on challenging gender issues, and slowly enlarge the community itself. The WeChat groups provide an online platform to support each other as a community. The products we design also deliver empowering messages while financially support the project as the single source.



CinemQ


CINEMQ是根植上海的一个地下酷儿集合体。我们定期组织活动，聚焦中国及东亚酷儿影像文化，呈献来自世界各地的作品。

CINEMQ is an unrefined queer underground collective based in Shanghai. We organise events and curate content from around the world, with a focus on Chinese and East Asian queer screen culture.



放映活动

SCREENINGS


每月一次的放映活动中，我们将短片、影像和音乐混剪，集合世界酷儿影线的各色肮脏与艳魅。活动更是被TimeOut杂志评为上海最佳酷儿派对系列之一。放映免费向大众开放，淡妆浓抹则是必须。

We pull together the filthy and gorgeous of queer world cinema once a month: an eclectic mixtape of short films, visuals and music. Named one of Shanghai’s best queer parties by TimeOut. Screenings are always open to all. Glitter is required.



ZINE

Our bilingual English and Chinese platform publishes weekly articles from around the world on queer cinema and screen culture, with a focus on China and East Asia. Read it on our website and on Wechat, or get in touch to contribute.
