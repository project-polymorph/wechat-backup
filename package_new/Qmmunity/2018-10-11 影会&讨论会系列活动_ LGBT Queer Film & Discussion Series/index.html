活动概况 / About

"

HAPPY TOGETHER 观影会是由Qmmunity旗下的HAPPY TOGETHER集体所组织的电影放映活动，每个月的第二个周日在上海放映LGBT主题影片，借助影片展示群体中各种多元视角，并鼓励观众通过对话启发思想，促进包容。活动形式为先观影，后讨论；讨论无固定主题，参与者可以以电影主要思想、选角、情节历史背景、配乐、相关文学作品等为切入点，畅所欲言。我们邀请你抽出时间，和酷儿同仁们静下心共享一部好片，向生活压力、社会压力说不，为彼此创造出包容、安全的空间，通过影片的视角加深对自我和群体的认同。                       

 -Xiao Xuan


HAPPY TOGETHER is an LGBTQ+ screening and discussion series that occurs every second Sunday of the month, in Shanghai. It is organized by HAPPY TOGETHER COLLECTIVE, powered by Qmunnity. The series seeks to foster thoughtful and inclusive dialogue around films that showcase diverse queer experiences. Each screening will be followed by an open discussion on the month's film, which can include its themes, casting choice, historical context, soundtrack, and related literature. In these contemporary times, watching a movie with fellow queers can be at once a gesture of slowing down, refusing our oppressions, and a way to hold a little more space for each other, to see ourselves refracted and reflected back. 

-Xiao Xuan”

主持人介绍 / Hosts


Xiao Xuan

Xiao Xuan/ 主持人、活动组织者：酷儿诗人、音乐家、发型师；祖籍上海，长期定居加拿大，近年返沪寻求进一步发展。她的诗作以乌托邦、爱书、情感的温柔和和欲望的克制为主题。当前，她在撰写著作，从理论角度探索作家的个人经历对他们所著的文学、文化评论的影响。同时，她还是一名优秀的民谣吉他音乐创作者。


Xiao Xuan / Sherry is a queer femme poet, musician, and barber. Her past works have been manifested through notions of utopia, love speech, tenderness, and reticence. She is currently working on a book of auto-theory, and makes shimmering music through guitar and vocals. She is an immigrant-settler to Canada, and is presently based out of her hometown, Shanghai.

妮娜, Marina

妮娜 Marina/主持人、活动组织者:  是一名生活在上海的西班牙酷儿人士、喜剧演员，爱好包括听音乐、打游戏。自2014年起参与马德里国际LGBT电影节LesGaiCineMad组织工作。现在她希望找到志同道合者，一起助力上海的酷儿电影界和酷儿社群发展壮大。


Marina, Host/ Co-Organizer:  is a Spanish queer living in Shanghai. She’s been working since 2014 for LesGaiCineMad (Madrid’s International LGBT Film Festival) and now she’s looking forward to joining forces to promote the queer film scene in China. A comic, music and video game lover who’s keen to do her bit in Shanghai’s queer community.

沈睿立, Riley

Riley主持人、电影爱好者: 是一名在上海已生活了三年的加拿大酷儿人士，拥有劳动研究、劳动法专业学术背景，在来华任教前，曾先后于一公共部门劳工组织和一电力公司任职。在教学中，他常鼓励学生质疑一切，勇于进行批判性思考。他的爱好包括打LOL、享用烤芝士三明治、以及享受足底按摩。


Riley is a Canadian queer who has been living in Shanghai for over three years. He studied labour studies and labour law and previously worked for a public service union and for an electricity company prior to coming to teach in China.  His interests include League of Legends, grilled cheeses, foot massages and encouraging his students to question everything and develop their critical thinking.

HAPPY TOGETHER集体致力于为每一个人创造安全的空间。电影排片表未来可能发生调整，以更充分反映并尊重酷儿群体中存在的多元视角。活动不支持参与者使用歧视、压迫性言行，鼓励大家在讨论中互相尊重，互相倾听。

HAPPY TOGETHER COLLECTIVE is devoted to holding a safe space for everyone. The schedule of movies is tentative; we all have different lived experiences which are all valid, and as such, the collective will do our best to have our screenings reflect that range. We will not tolerate oppressivebehavior. Please listen and participate with respect for each other. 

- Xiao Xuan



关于我们的名字HAPPY TOGETHER：这个名字反映了我们的心愿——与酷儿群体中的成员共享欢乐观影时光。由于单词gay在英文中的释义之一是“欢乐、快乐”，英文词happy一直以来都与酷儿们有着微妙的关系。曾经，英语国家的人们常用gay一词辱骂酷儿人士，随后又以该词的“欢乐”释义狡辩自己并无恶意。HAPPY TOGETHER代表着我们坚信酷儿人士可以获得真正、长久、切实的幸福快乐。同时，HAPPY TOGETHER还是王家卫1977年电影《春光乍洩》的英文名。该片将于2018年10月14日的活动中放映。

- Xiao Xuan


A note on the namesake: HAPPY TOGETHER takes its name after the experience we want to facilitate, that is, one of collective joy! As we know, happy's meaning has historically been fraught in its relationship to queerness. It evokes the phrase which usually follows being called gay in a nasty, homophobic way, "I meant gay as in happy." So gay's lexical meaning has always been used as an excuse for what is actually hate speech. HAPPY TOGETHER is an insistence that gay can indeed mean happy, and that that happiness can be genuine, lasting, and rooted firmly in the here and now. It is also the English translation of Wong Kar Wai's 1977 film, 春光乍洩, the first film of the series, which will be screening October 14th, 2018.   

- Xiao Xuan
