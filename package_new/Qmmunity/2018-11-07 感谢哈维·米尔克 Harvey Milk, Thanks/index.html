纪念哈维·米尔克

Celebrating Harvey Milk


1977年11月8日，哈维·米尔克经历三轮选举，成功担任旧金山监督委员会成员，成为了美国加利福尼亚州历史上第一名在民选中胜出，当选公职的公开同性恋者，开创了美国社会接纳包容LGBT人士的先河。为此，虹社于今年11月8日特举办电影《米尔克》放映会，以向LGBT历史中的这位伟人致敬。

Harvey Milk paved the way for LGBTQ acceptance on his path to being the first openly gay elected official in the history of California. November 8, 1977 Harvey Milk was finally elected to the San Francisco Board of Supervisors after his third campaign. That’s why we are gathering together as a Qmmunity to watch the movie Milk on November 8, and take a moment to say thank you, to one of the great contributors of our history.


哈维·米尔克让人们看到了粉色经济的力量

Harvey Milk taught us the power of the 

Pink Economy. 

在哈维·米尔克定居至旧金山卡斯特罗区前，当地仍有部分商家歧视  LGBTQ顾客。米尔克团结起了卡斯特罗区的LGBTQ人士，统一抵制这些商家，最终使得它们门可罗雀，只得关门大吉。相比之下，包容LGBTQ的商家则未受到影响。

When Harvey Milk moved to the Castro District in San Francisco, there were still businesses in the neighborhood who were anti-LGBTQ. Harvey Milk organized the community and made sure that they all stopped shopping at the businesses that were anti-LGBTQ. By doing so, the ones who were anti-LGBTQ shut down, and the businesses that were accepting, stayed in business. 


通过这一事件，人们看到了LGBTQ群体的庞大购买力，而LGBTQ人士也体会到了团结的力量。只要我们凝聚社群力量，必能让社会更加包容——无论过去还是现在，这一真理永不改变。

This method helped show the power our spending has, and when organized we can make a difference in creating a more welcoming world. The same still holds true today. 


哈维·米尔克促进了主流社会对我们的认识

Harvey Milk helped the mainstream understand us. 

米尔克是第一名在民选中胜出，当选公职的公开同性恋者，也是第一名公开捍卫LGBT权益，引导社会认真讨论LGBT群体的政治人物。

As the first openly elected official, he was the first openly gay politician campaigning against and starting the dialogue about what it means to be LGBTQ. 

“1977年迈阿密（发生地方反歧视同性恋法规被废除）事件，让同性恋人士失去了不少权利。但请注意：迈阿密事件爆发的前后共两周时间内，全美国的每一份报纸、每一家电视台、每一个广播站都对同性恋话题进行了报道，每家每户每个人都在讨论，可谓史无前例。有支持我们的声音，也有反对我们的声音，但如果没有这种讨论，没有这种对话，人们的观念就无法改变。短短两周内产生的关于同性恋群体的报道和言论量，已经超过了人类历史上的总和。只要讨论开始了，距离消灭偏见的那一天也就不远了。”

——节选自米尔克的“希望”演说



“In 1977, gay people had their rights taken away from them in Miami. But you must remember that in the week before Miami and the week after that, the word homosexual or gay appeared in every single newspaper in this nation in articles both pro and con. In every radio station, in every TV station and every household. For the first time in the history of the world, everybody was talking about it, good or bad. Unless you have dialogue, unless you open the walls of dialogue, you can never reach to change people's opinion. In those two weeks, more good and bad, but more about the word homosexual and gay was written than probably in the history of mankind. Once you have dialogue starting, you know you can break down prejudice.” 

-Milk in his “Hope” speech 


米尔克呼吁全美国所有的LGBTQ人士向周围的人出柜，开始了一场全国性的出柜运动，打破主流社会和LGBT群体之间的隔阂和误解，让主流社会对LGBTQ的认识不再停留在抽象——LGBTQ群体是由一个个鲜活的个人组成的，也许就是你我身边的家人、朋友、同事。

He started America’s coming out movement, and asked the community all over the United States to come out. By doing so, it helped break down barriers and misconceptions because it personalized it, and helped the mainstream realize that they had friends, family, and co-workers that were part of our community.



“同性恋兄弟姐妹们，你们必须出柜。向家人出柜吧！我知道出柜很困难，家人会很受伤，但是如果你不这么做，最终他们必将在投票时伤害你的权益。向朋友出柜吧！这才是友谊的真正考验。向亲戚、向邻居、向同事、向你每日光顾的商店餐馆服务员出柜吧！用尽一切努力，向所有与你相识的人出柜，打破他们的误解，粉碎那些谎言，让他们看到事实真相！这是为了你自己好，也是为了他们好。”

——哈维·米尔克鼓励人们出柜的发言



“Gay brothers and sisters, you must come out. Come out to your parents. I know that it is hard and will hurt them, but think about how they will hurt you in the voting booth! Come out to your relatives. Come out to your friends, if indeed they are your friends. Come out to your neighbors, to your fellow workers, to the people who work where you eat and shop. Come out only to the people you know, and who know you, not to anyone else. But once and for all, break down the myths. Destroy the lies and distortions. For your sake. For their sake.”

-Harvey Milk encouraging people to come out 



哈维·米尔克为我们创造了标志性的彩虹旗

Milk gave us a symbol. 


在哈维·米尔克的委托和启发下，设计师Gilbert Baker创造出了象征LGBT骄傲的彩虹旗。1978年6月25日的旧金山同性恋自由日游行中，彩虹旗第一次在人群上空飘扬。

Harvey Milk challenged Gilbert Baker to come up with a symbol of pride for the gay community. Gilbert Baker came up with the rainbow flag, and the original gay pride flag flew at the San Francisco Gay Freedom Day Parade celebration on June 25, 1978. 


1978年11月27日，米尔克遇刺。随后，彩虹旗的销量大幅上升。

After Milk’s assassination on November 27, 1978, demand for the rainbow flag greatly increased.

哈维·米尔克给我们带来了希望

Harvey Milk gave us hope. 



哈维·米尔克已经成为全世界LGBTQ群体的代表人物。他用言行告诉我们，只要勇于接受真实自己，勇于改变社会现状，必能最终获得包容。

Harvey Milk became a symbol for our people all over the world. That when we embrace our identity, and aren’t afraid to challenge the status quo, we can be accepted. 


哈维·米尔克告诉我们世界在不断进步，社会一定会朝着越来越包容的方向前进。

Harvey Milk gave hope that the world was moving, and continues to move towards a more accepting place.


正在阅读这篇文章的你，请一定不要失去希望。也许你现在不能出柜，但别忘了社群里有许多人在默默努力，愿有朝一日能为你创造更开放包容的环境。

If you’re reading this, stay hopeful. Even if you’re not able to come out, there are people working behind the scenes to help make it more of a possibility.
