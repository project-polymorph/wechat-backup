同性夫妇的家庭建设选择

LGBTQ+ Friendly Family Planning

     同性夫妇和变性人面临着独特的生育环境，往往必须从各种生殖渠道中做出选择。例如，女同性恋夫妇选择一个精子捐献者，可以选择子宫内受精（IUI），其中一方使用她的卵子完成受孕；或者可以选择体外受精（IVF），其中一方捐献她的卵子，另一个人负责怀孕。而男同性恋者既需要一个卵子捐献者，也需要一个载体。变性为男子的人如果在变性手术中仍保留了原来的性别生殖器官，那么是可以生育的，但是如果生殖器官也做了变性手术，那就意味着没有生育的能力了。


     由此可见，在同性夫妇和变性人不断增多的社会环境下，试管技术不单单是不孕不育，生育困难家庭的选择，这些特殊人群的生育愿望，也可以通过一系列的辅助生殖技术来实现。


LGBTQ people face unique reproductive challenges and often have to choose from a variety of channels that can recurrently include Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART). For instance, this could be a lesbian couple opting to choose a sperm donor for Intrauterine Fertilization (IUI), in which one partner uses her eggs to conceive. Another example is In Vitro Fertilization (IVF), in which one partner donates her eggs and the other carries out the pregnancy. However, a male gay couple would need both an egg donor and a carrier, or surrogate.


These options used to be seen as services only heterosexual couples with fertility issues would pursue, but luckily for the LGBTQ+ community, they are becoming less stigmatized and more widely available for us to realize our family planning dreams.


辅助生育专家的莅临

Assisted Reproduction Experts

     在此次的研讨会上，我们可以听到专业辅助生育专家的意见，分享来自于世界各地的关于同性伴侣，异性伴侣，单身男性和单身女性的辅助生育方案。同时我们将广泛讨论不同辅助生殖方案的定价、医疗信息服务和与之相关的法律。


At this workshop, we will have the privilege to hear the opinions and witness the expertise of Assisted Reproduction Specialists. They will educate us on the assisted reproduction services available to LGBTQ+ families, but also single men and women, and heterosexual couples. We will also discuss in detail the pricing of all these services, as well as news and legalities surrounding them.
