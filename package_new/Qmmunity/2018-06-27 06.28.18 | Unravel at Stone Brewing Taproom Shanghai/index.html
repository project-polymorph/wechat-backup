06.28.18 | Unravel at Stone Brewing Taproom Shanghai

StoneBrewing Qmmunity
2018年06月27日 20:16

6月28日，Stone Brewing将首次与Unravel合作举办6月的“故事大会”。届时所有的分享者及客人将第一次踏入即将在7月揭幕的Stone精酿酒吧餐厅，进行一场独特的幕后体验。


作为沪上知名的真实故事现场分享平台, Unravel六月的主题THIS IS ME将围绕“全球骄傲”，协同LesQueers, PFLAG和ShanghaiPRIDE，一起支持LGBTQ社群及相关项目的发展。我们将与Unravel一起倡导包容与平等，无论你的出身，外貌，立场及性取向。


与Unravel以往的活动一样，这一期同样欢迎任何人参与及聆听。如果你有意分享自己独特的亲历故事，只需将你的想法或问题发送至：unravelshanghai@gmail.com. (分享以英文为主，配合少量中文翻译。）


Stone Brewing将所有当晚盈利的10%捐赠给PFLAG。

(Unravel往期活动照片，由摄影师Alejandro Scott提供）


We’re thrilled to announce that Stone Brewing Taproom Shanghai will team up with Unravel, a unique storytelling platform, to host its monthly show on June 28. Guests and storytellers will get a first look at the soon-to-debut, first-of-its-kind taproom launching in July 2018. A company that values independence and integrity, Stone Brewing has a legacy of bucking convention and leading with quality. 


This month Unravel is also teaming up with LesQueers, supported by PFLAG and ShanghaiPride, for its June theme: THIS IS ME. For its second annual celebration of the global Pride movement, Unravel asserts its firm commitment to inclusion and visibility for all, regardless of where you come from, how you identify or present, what you look like, or who you love. 


Like any of its shows, this event is open to all people, all stories, and all interpretations of the theme. Submit your story or learn more by emailing unravelshanghai@gmail.com.


Stone Brewing will donate 10% of the profit from the event to PFLAG.

Unravel


Unrael是一个独特的月度故事分享平台。每一期讲述者都将根据月度主题，讲述他们人生的真实经历。每一期活动旨在创造将场地，主题和分享者完美结合的体验。

Unravel的灵感来自上海这个多彩的城市，每天都有不可思议的奇妙故事发生在我们身边。我们重视社区，联结及影响力。我们相信每个人都有一个（甚至无数个）值得分享的好故事。

Unravel is a storytelling platform, premised on a monthly live show. At each show, a group of storytellers recount authentic stories from their own lives loosely inspired by a given theme. Each live show is a unique combination of venue, theme, and storyteller lineup.

Unravel was created to serve as a platform for some of the incredible stories and characters that make Shanghai, China such a vibrant place to live and work. We value community, connection, and reflection. And we firmly believe everyone has a story (or hundreds of stories) to tell.

LesQueers


LesQueers是一个推动及支持LGBTQIA社群的平台。他们开发的手机app创全球之首，旨在将个人与社群有机结合。你可以选择世界任何城市，找到对LGBTQ友好的场所。


LesQueers is an LGBTQIA+ empowerment platform, community, and movement. Their recently launched mobile application is the first of its kind: connecting the community through both individual or organization and business accounts. Select a city or a country to find the queer happenings of any place in the world. 


PFLAG

今年是PFLAG中国成立10周年的纪念，在全国已有超过3000名志愿成员（其中1000名为LGBTQ社群的父母）。通过交流沟通，深入了解，互助及讲座，将LGBTQ社群父母及其家庭成员结合在一起，增加社群影响力。

This year marks the 10th anniversary of PFLAG China, an organization that focuses on parents and families in China accepting their LGBTQ+ family member. Powered by a volunteer force 3,000 strong across the country (1,000 of which are parents), they facilitate dialogues, exchanges, helplines, lectures and other activities to support their mission of acceptance and awareness. 

ShanghaiPRIDE


作为上海LGBTQ的首要倡导组织之一，ShanghaiPRIDE同样在今年迎来其成立10周年。通过媒体，商界，社团及外国领馆的大力支持，以及由志愿者组织的大型体育竞技，文化等社交活动，ShanghaiPRIDE的年度庆典正在吸引越来越多人关注LGBTQ群体的自我认知及健康发展。


Also celebrating a decade of action and advocacy, ShanghaiPRIDE is an annual festival that celebrates diversity, organized entirely by volunteers with the support of media, businesses, communities, and foreign consulates.  The mission is to raise awareness and visibility, and to promote self-acceptance and acceptance for the LGBTQ community through sports, cultural, and social activities.
