制定友好的企业规章制度

Policy & Procedure

在企业的招聘规章中，明确体现对各种性取向和性身份的认可和包容——表明企业认可性少数群体的存在，并接受性少数员工。

Make sure that your business has and specifically states sexual orientation & gender identity in your hiring policy: specifically stating it helps recognize that the community exists and is accepted within your company. 


入职培训期间，向新员工强调企业欢迎来自多元背景的人士，特别是LGBT人士。这样的对话能让LGBT新员工知道企业文化对自己是包容的，并获得出柜的勇气。

During the employee on-boarding process emphasize that diversity is welcomed and specifically mention LGBT when talking discussing that. When you initiate the dialogue it’s letting the queer community know that your culture is accepting, which may allow for the employee to come out.


制定防骚扰规章制度以保护性少数员工，并让管理层所有人都知道如何处理员工骚扰事件。

Create a clear anti-harassment policy and procedure and teach your managers how to respond to these situations 


应保证LGBT员工和他们的伴侣也能享受企业的福利。在中国，同性恋婚姻是不被认可的，但是如果你的LGBT员工有伴侣，则他们的伴侣应享有与异性伴侣同等的福利。

Make sure your company benefits include LGBT people and their partners. Marriage equality does not exist in China; however if your employee has a partner, offer them the same benefits that exist for your married heterosexual employees.


为什么要这么做？

Why does this matter?


中国只有4%的LGBT人士已出柜。而企业是一种重要的力量，可推动社会变得更加包容，让每个人都释放真实自我。

With only 4% of the total LGBT community in China being out, company’s play an integral role in creating an environment where people can truly be themselves.  



贴上安全空间标识

Safe Marker


在办公室墙上、门口、接待处挂上彩虹标识，就会让LGBT员工和客户知道你的企业对性少数群体是友好的。

A rainbow marker anywhere in the office, on the door, at reception: sends the message to LGBT employees or customers that you are LGBT friendly.  


社群成员向静安区某餐馆店主介绍Q Key标示   

Explaining the Q Key to a restaurant owner in Jing'an


为什么要这么做？

Why does this matter?


Qmmunity虹社最开始向上海静安区的商家挨家挨户向发放Q Key标识（LGBT友好标识）时，遭到了20%的商家的拒绝。

When Qmmunity started distributing its Q Key stickers around Shanghai, going door to door in Jing’an District, 20% of businesses said no. 



开展多元性和包容性培训 

Diversity & Inclusion 

新西兰驻沪总领馆员工于2018年8月参与了中国的第一场多元性和包容性培训，针对性促进对LGBT群体的包容。培训项目由虹社提供。

New Zealand Consulate General staff in Shanghai participating in China’s first official LGBT specific Diversity & Inclusion Training program by Qmmunity, August 2018



如果在企业内部对员工进行多元性和包容性培训，则应保证培训内容涵盖LGBT人群。通过培训，企业的管理层和员工就会知道如何更好地让企业文化更具包容性。

If you enroll your company in a diversity & inclusion training, make sure the training covers LGBT. Training helps educate your management and staff on better practices for creating an inclusive culture. 


为什么要这么做？

Why does this matter?


歧视往往来源于无知。中国的教育体系中没有任何关于LGBT群体的知识。在西方，学校里都会有GSA（直人酷儿联盟会），但中国学校（除非是国际学校）禁止讨论LGBT话题。你的企业所开展的多元性和包容性培训，很有可能是员工人生中第一次正式接触LGBT相关知识和信息。

Discrimination often stems from ignorance. There is little to no formal education about LGBT issues and topics. Unlike the west where schools have GSAs, China often does not allow LGBT topics in schools (except for International schools). If your company takes a diversity & inclusion training it could be the first formal introduction to LGBT that your staff has ever had. 




招聘LGBT员工

Recruit LGBT Employees

参加LGBT人士招聘会寻找人才：WorkforLGBT和Qmmunity这两个组织都会举办针对LGBT人士的招聘会

Attend LGBT Hiring Conferences: WorkforLGBT and Qmmunity both organize different events for hiring LGBT Employees. 


若你有意在LGBT群体中进行招聘，请给Qmmunity微信公众号留言，我们会邀请您进入LGBT求职群。

There are WeChat groups that exist for specifically recruiting LGBT employees. You can private message us on our official account to be added to these groups.


为什么要这么做？

Why does this matter?


在LGBT人群中进行招聘，可以明确体现你的企业的包容性和多元性。在中国，企业可充分挖掘LGBT群体求职平台，招贤纳士，提升企业内部多元性。

Recruiting in LGBT specific spaces sends the clear message that your company is inclusive and values diversity. There are resources available in China for targeting LGBT employees, use them to add diversity to your team. 



赞助LGBT组织或活动

Sponsor an LGBT Organization or Event 

2018 LGBT社区启动大会   

 2018 LGBT Shanghai Events Kickoff 


中国的大城市里LGBT群体往往较为活跃，上海尤其如此。企业赞助LGBT群体的活动，不仅能帮助到活动组织者，还能让群体增强对你的品牌的认同。

The LGBT scenes in larger cities are quite active. The Shanghai LGBT scene is particularly dynamic. Sponsorship doesn’t only help the organizers, but it also creates brand recognition amongst the LGBT community.


为什么要这么做？

Why does this matter?


在中国社会，主流群体的意见是很重要的。企业作为主流社会的成员，在中国支持对于LGBT群体的讨论，就能促使大环境更加包容，让多元背景人士进入主流。许多的LGBT活动都是由社群人士自掏腰包举办的；企业的赞助能帮助LGBT这个被遗忘的群体更频繁地举办高质量的活动。而群体中的成员在未来做出消费选择时，也会首先想到曾经赞助过活动的商家。

Chinese society values the mainstream, when corporations are able to support LGBT topics in China: it send a strong message about acceptance, and normalizes diversity.  Also the organizers of these events and organizations are often doing so with their own capital, sponsorship helps creates stronger programming for a neglected demographic— and the community will remember your brand when making their purchasing decisions later. 



根据LGBT消费者讯息网站LGBT Capital的估计，在中国LGBT群体总人口9000万，每年创造的GDP高达9800亿美元。企业凭借自己在社会中的独特地位，可以通过商业活动让中国的社会环境大为不同。体现对LGBT群体的友好，不仅能展示品牌的包容精神，还能为企业带来可观的经济效益。

LGBT Capital estimates that the annual LGBT GDP in China is 980 Billion USD, with an LGBT population of over 90 million. Businesses are in a unique and privileged position to make a big difference while doing business in China. Becoming LGBT Friendly isn’t just branding yourself as an inclusive company, it’s good for your bottom line.
