原创 LQ Qmmunity

出发吃喝玩乐前，我们会查看攻略，搜索网络评价，询问朋友等……但似乎一直以来，很少有一种方式能够直接告诉LGBTQIA+人群，哪里是更包容更友好的场所。

Before we go out for a brunch, dinner, or a casual lunch, we usually ask for suggestions from our friends. However, there are few channels available for the LGBTQIA+ community to find proper places that are inclusive and friendly.


LesQueers（下文简称LQ）为了帮助社群寻找并拓宽酷儿友好消费空间，在6月3日联合同性恋亲友会上海分会成功开展了彩虹指南项目，共集结了15位可爱的彩虹友好商户行动大使们（下文简称彩虹大使）为50多家企业及商铺贴上了彩虹标识。

In order to help the community find safe spaces, LesQueers (LQ), created the Q Key, a guide to finding LGBTQIA+ friendly spaces. LQ cooperated with PFLAG Shanghai to successfully launch the Q Key Initiative on Sunday, June 3. 15 rainbow ambassadors gathered together to successfully dispatch over 50 rainbow stickers to businesses and stores in Shanghai.

什么是彩虹指南？ 

What is the Q Key? 


彩虹标识源自LQ提出的彩虹指南（Q Key），是中国第一份酷儿必备的生活就业全攻略，从社群需求出发，让每一位性少数群体用最简单的方式发现最适合自己的去处。

The Q Key system launched by LQ serves as China’s first comprehensive guidance about life and employment for the queer community. The system enables the LGBTQIA+ to quickly find safe spaces in a simple and convenient way, built into a mobile application that integrates with all aspects of daily life.



彩虹标识由LQ设计，背景色为彩虹色，代表彩虹社区的全部性别/性取向身份人群，标识上的5个Q Key，分别从五个维度全面评估一个商户的彩虹友好程度。

The sticker, designed by LQ, chose a rainbow background to represent the beautiful diversity that exists within our spectrum of gender and sexual identities. The five Q Keys work as an objective guide to help the community evaluate a business from five different dimensions.


谈到性少数群体标志性的彩虹，LQ创始人Gabby Gabriel谈了她自己的见解：“彩虹是世界上最伟大的团结力量之一，无论它开始于何处，它时刻都传递着这一含义：欢迎你来这里。"

Gabby Gabriel believes, “The rainbow is one of the world’s greatest unifying forces – for no matter where it is found, it carries the same meaning: you are welcome here.”



谈到彩虹指南的第一次活动，Gabby分享道：“上海对LQ有着非凡的意义，是LQ的开始，这也是为什么我们把彩虹指南的起点安排在了上海。同时，在7月，彩虹指南也将登陆美国及南美，将社群的力量回馈给社群，我们希望让更多人享受自如选择的权利。”

When it came to the first rainbow campaign, Gabby told us that, “Shanghai possesses an extraordinary significance to LQ, this wonderful place is where we began, that’s why we chose Shanghai as the starting point of our rainbow campaign. Soon afterwards, the campaign will launch in the United States and in South America later this summer. Using technology to empower our community, we hope that more people will be free to be who they truly are.”



在活动过程中，向商户负责人介绍LQ中国APP拥有的展示商铺功能通常不是最困难的部分，酷儿文化及LGBT所代表的含义才是需要花时间沟通的重头戏。

During the campaign, communicating the technology of LQ isn’t the most challenging process, instead it is bridging the learning curve about the LGBTQIA+ commmunity that requires the most time and efforts.


咖啡店店员：二次“斗争”后，老板终于同意贴上彩虹标识 

Coffee Shop Clerk: After a bit of back and forth, the store owner finally agreed 


在活动过程中，如果遇到老板不在的情况，彩虹大使们能做的就是向店员讲解然后留下彩虹标识并等待后续，其中有一家咖啡店的后续给我们留下深刻印象。

During the campaign process, if ambassadors encountered the absence of store owners, the only thing they could do is explain to clerks and leave the rainbow stickers, waiting for a follow-up. After visiting over one hundred stores, there was one coffee shop that left an impactful impression. 



彩虹大使Moyi告诉我们：“6月3日在活动的过程中我们给一家咖啡馆留下了彩虹标识，店员表示会和老板商量后决定是否贴上。第二天，店员回复我们协商失败，没办法贴上彩虹标识。但在5号的第二次回复里，她说在经过‘斗争’后，终于可以贴上彩虹标识了。在之后的交流中得知，她也是酷儿社群的一员，她身边也有很多酷儿朋友，希望大家能够加油坚持下去。”

Rainbow ambassador, Moyi, told us about an encounter with a coffee shop. The clerk initially wouldn’t allow Moyi & her team to put on a safe-space sticker, since the owner wasn’t at the shop. Moyi left the sticker with the clerk, with hopes the clerk would get back to her. The next day, the clerk said she couldn’t persuade her boss to put up the sticker. However, five days later, the clerk followed up with Moyi & LQ again, to say that the boss had a change of heart. Later LQ was informed that actually the clerk is part of the queer community, and has lots of queer friends. The clerk hopes that we will continue doing what we are doing.


彩虹大使与商户协商

Negotiation with the business owner


纹身店店主：我不了解酷儿文化，但我愿意去了解 

Tattoo Shop Owner: I don’t know about queer culture, but I’m willing to learn 


酷儿文化作为亚文化，不是所有人都知道是正常的。但如果每个人都愿意保持开放的态度去了解去接纳，那世界是不是又变得彩虹了一点呢？

Queer culture is not yet mainstream. However, if society keeps an open mind and learns to accept it: could our rainbow help make the world shine more brightly?


在经过一家纹身店的时候，起初彩虹大使们在门口犹豫徘徊，但店内的店员们主动上前友好地询问情况，“在我们介绍后依然非常热情地表示支持，并互相留了微信结交成为了朋友。”彩虹大使阿阮说。

Rainbow ambassador Aruan said, “We were lingering outside a tattoo shop, the clerks friendlily greeted us and asked about our purpose. They showed great compassion and support after we introduced our initiative. We even exchanged social media to keep in touch with each other.”
