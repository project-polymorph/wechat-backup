什么是巴恰塔（Bachata) ?
What is Bachata?

巴恰塔是一种浪漫的拉丁舞蹈，和萨尔萨舞一样充满了性感与火辣。巴恰塔舞蹈对于初学者来说也因为它较缓慢的节奏和简单的左右摆动动作而易于上手。
Similar to Salsa, Bachata is a romantic Latin style dance with a sensual and sexy feel. With its relatively slow pace and simple side-to-side and forward-to-back stepping, this intimate dance is perfect for beginners.



 
为什么要学习巴恰塔舞？
Why learn Bachata? 

巴恰塔舞现在已经成为风靡全球的舞蹈之一。究其原因也很简单：跳巴恰塔舞有益于健康，还能瘦身提臀。通过参加Hot Salsa俱乐部的课程，还能得到认识新朋友的绝佳机会。快来加入我们这个大家庭吧。
Bachata has become the most popular Latin dance around the world, and it’s easy to see why! Dancing Bachata is a great way to get healthy, lose weight and to loosen up your hips, and attending a Bachata class at Hot Salsa club will give you the opportunity to make new friends from all walks of life, and to feel like part of a big family.

 



课程详情
Class Information

我们欢迎所有来自LGBTQIA+社群的朋友们来参加，当然我们的课程也会对直女开放。本课程暂不对直男学生开放。本课程旨在让初学者享受到跳巴恰塔舞带来的乐趣，当然如果你已经有了一定的经验，我们也欢迎你来秀一秀舞姿！
All members of the LGBTQIA+ community are welcome to attend, and the class is also open to straight women. Hot Salsa Club is unable to accommodate straight men for this class. Classes are aimed at beginners - although those with Bachata experience are more than welcome to come down to brush up on their skills and to review the steps!
 
在课程中，我们会一起准备圣诞派对的演出。不过，课程目标依然是学习一全套巴恰塔舞的舞步，并不要求学员一定要参加最终的演出。
During the classes, you will work towards preparing for a Christmas party performance. However, the main aim of the class is to learn the full set of Bachata dancing steps, and it is not a requirement for class attendees to take part in the performance.
