Qmmunity 是什么？

    What does Qmmunity mean?



Queer + Community  = Qmmunity



 新名字怎么念？

     How do you say it?


发音就是 Community。没错，我们叫它 Community，但是拼写为 Qmmunity。


Community   — that’s right, we say it the same as Community — but we just spell it Qmmunity. 



 为什么要改名？

    Why?


因为 Qmmunity 为每个人服务，

我们希望它的名字也能反映这一点。


Qmmunity is for everyone, 

and we wanted a name that reflects that. 



Qmmunity 是为每个人服务吗？ 

    
     Is Qmmunity for everyone? 


我们希望让 LGBTQIA+ 成为主流。

We want to make LGBTQIA+ mainstream. 


这意味着所有社群里的成员：同盟和那些认为自己是酷儿的一份子，都被欢迎成为我们组织的一部分。我们一直强调说，我们永远把自己人放在第一位，我们为你而存在。我们的特别群组和 App 中的一部分内容，都是我们专属的。


That means all people of the community: allies, and those who identify as part of the Q, are welcome as part of our organization. With that being said, we put our people first. 

We are here for you.  We have special groups, and areas on our app, that are only for us. 


相互性是实现我们使命的关键。在这个使命中，人们不应只被期望为异性恋者或顺性别者。

Intersectionality is key towards accomplishing our mission — where people aren’t expected to only be heterosexual or CIS-gendered.
