关于国际不再恐同日

ABOUT IDAHOT


创立于2004年，国际不再恐同日旨在唤起政界、社会团体、媒体以及社会大众对LGBTQ人士恐惧与暴力的意识。

The International Day Against Homophobia, Transphobia and Biphobia was created in 2004 in order to raise awareness among politicians, social movements, media and the general public about the violence and discrimination that LGBTQ people suffer around the world.

经过时间洗礼与多年积累发展，5月17日国际不再恐同日俨然已经 然成为国际社会上最重要的LGBTQ活动。这一重要日子无不彰显出着全球范围内社群同胞及友好人士对女同、男同、双性恋、跨性别、双性别以及其他所有有别于传统性别与性取向人士的存在意义、权利的捍卫与提高大众意识所付出的泪与汗。

Throughout these years, the 17th of May has become the most important LGBTQ movement at an international level. The day represents the importance of defending and raising consciousness of the situation of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and intersexual people across the globe, as well as all those who don't feel identified with any of the heteronormativity tags in sexual or gender terms.



关于虹社

ABOUT QMMUNITY


成立于2004年，Qummunity（中文名虹社）致力于为中国LGBTQ人士创造一个安全、团结并且充满人权的社会空间。自成立以来，虹社不断帮助中国本土人士与在华外国人士发声，在其危难之时伸出援手提供心灵庇护所，并且通过定期举办的研讨与活动向社群内外积极宣传LGBTQ权利，树立正确价值观。虹社在中国各大城市有超过一万名的社群成员，包括但不限于上海、成都、广州、济南、北京。作为LGBTQ社群先锋，虹社成功向中国社会各界宣传LGBTQ价值观，更创办了具有非凡历史意义的LGBTQ Diversity & Inclusion项目。

Founded in 2014, Qmmunity,  has the goal of creating a safe, unifying and empowering space for LGBTQ people in China. Since its creation, Qmmunity has been helping Chinese and foreigners find their own voice within the community, find support throughout their struggles and teach about LGBTQ values through all kind of workshops, events. Qmmunity has over ten thousand members spread throughout different cities in China: Shanghai, Chengdu, Guangzhou, Jinan, and Beijing. They also have had meetings with officials to advocate on behalf of the LGBTQ community. Qmmunity also created China’s first LGBTQ Diversity & Inclusion program.
