6月21日，「LQ中国」APP在北京WeWork与大家见面，LQ的创始人们在会上向来到现场的20多位来宾介绍了「LQ中国」APP是LQ助力改变社群环境的一项重要举措，通过APP你能够及时了解最新的社群活动，找到最友好的酷儿商铺，发现最值得工作的公司。

On June 21st, the Beijing soft APP launch successfully took place in the Beijing Guanghua Lu WeWork. The LQ founders introduced the LQ App to over 20 guests, as an important move for promoting the amelioration of the community environment. One can keep up with the latest community activities, find the most Queer friendly venues and companies through LQ.

LesBooze

LesBooze是为酷儿女性相见、相遇而举办的双月社交活动。同时也会有主题活动或者有特别主题的日间活动，例如今夏的“Les Get Wet”泳池派对。总的来说，我们的目标是为酷儿女性提供一个用于见面及社交并且安全有趣的空间。

LesBooze is a bi-monthly social event for queer women to meet, mix, and mingle. Occasionally, we have themed events or special daytime activities, such as this summer’s “Les Get Wet” pool party, but overall, our aim is simply to provide a safe space and fun environment for queer women to meet each other and socialize.



Q-Space

Q-Space是一家位于北京的酷儿，女权和创客空间。我们致力于通过各种活动和工作坊来构建多元的社区和文化。

Q-Space is a Queer, Feminist, and Maker space in Beijing, We devote ourselves to making diverse communities through workshops and various creative events.



LQ在京期间，北京的各大使馆也对LQ正在开展的事业展示出浓厚兴趣，LQ分别与加拿大、美国及德国大使馆的外交官员进行了亲切会晤。

The embassies in Beijing also showed great interest in the LQ’s pioneer undertaking. During the stay in Beijing, LQ had friendly meetings with the diplomatic officials of the Canadian Embassy, the U.S. Embassy and the German Embassy.
