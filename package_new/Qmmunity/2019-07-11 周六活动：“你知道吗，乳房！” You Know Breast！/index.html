周六活动：“你知道吗，乳房！” You Know Breast！

Qmmunity
2019年07月11日 07:45

本周六，裸月 (LUÜNA Naturals)，上海美中嘉和肿瘤门诊部，SoGal Ventures和虹社将要举办“你知道吗，乳房！”（You Know Breast）座谈会，希望能够在一个安全和温馨的环境中打破禁忌，理解乳房和身体。

This Saturday LUÜNA Naturals, Concord Medical Cancer Centre Shanghai, SoGal Ventures and Qmmunity are hosting You Know Breast, an event dedicated to the breaking of taboos and the understanding of breasts and our bodies in a safe and welcoming environment for all.




我们很荣幸能够邀请到五位出色的女性来参与本次座谈会：Olivia（裸月的创办人及行政总裁）、林琳医生（上海美中嘉和肿瘤门诊部的医学专家）、Gabby（虹社代表）、Pocket（SoGal Ventures的创办人及行政总裁）以及Jay Jay（淋巴瘤倖存者），我们将一起享受早午餐和鸡尾酒，她们还会跟我们分享她们对于女性健康的个人见解与建议。林琳医生还会告诉我们如何专业的检查自己的乳房健康状况。

Things to look forward to include a panel discussion and workshop, brunch, and cocktails! The panel is comprised of awesome female leaders: Olivia (LUÜNA CEO & founder), Dr. Lin Lin (from Concord Medical Cancer Center), Gabby (Qmmunity CEO & founder), Pocket (SoGal Ventures CEO & founder), and Jay Jay (badass Lymphoma survivor). They will offer their inspiring and honest insights on female health! Following the discussion there will be a Q&A with Dr. Lin Lin where she will teach us how to check our breasts and evaluate their health.


座谈会将在由女性企业家团队创建的金花云南菜馆举行，现在购买门票即可获得价值约700元的检查体验和来自裸月的一个月有机棉护理套装。

The workshop will be held at the amazing Jin Hua restaurant, founded by a crack team of fab female entrepreneurs.The 188 RMB ticket includes a mammogram (worth 700 RMB at Concord Medical Center), and a month’s worth of organic, chemical-free pads or tampons! Don’t miss out.
