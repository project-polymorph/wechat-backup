健康性爱是我们身体健康的重要组成部分之一。性健康的重要性体现在生活的方方面面，比如伴侣之间享受和谐性生活的能力，如何远离性传播感染，以及控制生育能力。LesQueers致力于为我们的社区内外成员收集相关资源，以提供给大家更安全的性保障措施。

Sexual health is an important part of overall health. The ability to enjoy fulfilling relationships, being free of sexually transmitted infections, and having the ability to control ones fertility are all aspects of good sexual health. LesQueers is committed to gathering information and safer sex strategies for our community and allies. 


为了帮助大家在遇到不同情境下可以第一时间找到靠谱合适的资源信息，我们为大家整理并制作了一个信息指南列表，你也可以在我们的网站以及LQ app的下一个版本中上找到这个列表。

In an effort to assist the community in finding the appropriate resources for varying situations, we created a guide, which we will keep on our website, and will also be part of the next version of our mobile application. 


请注意，我们还没有验证这些机构对于LGBTQIA+群体的友好程度，但其中部分机构是由LGBTQIA+社区人士运营。我们计划在接下来的两个月里对这些机构进行LGBT友好性的评估，并在之后再更新一篇文章。

Please note that we haven’t checked the LGBTQIA+ friendliness of these spaces, but we noted places on the list that are run by the community. We plan on conducting research about the LGBT friendliness of the different clinics over the next two months, and we will release an article after we have conducted our research.


Note: The cost for services for foreigners at local hospitals and clinics are generally a higher price due to the fact that they don't pay social security benefits to the government like the locals do.
